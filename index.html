Why GRID//NODE Exists

Protocol tracking is often spread across notes, screenshots, spreadsheets, calendars, and memory.

GRID//NODE begins with GLP-1 protocol tracking, then extends that record into a broader Personal Biotech Operating System for user-entered health protocol data.

Beyond GLP-1s, GRID//NODE organizes peptide research records, metabolic tracking, and user-entered protocol history — all in one place, all on your device.

The System

SHOTS

Record GLP-1 protocol events in a structured SHOTS history.

Log timing, locations, and notes so each user-entered SHOT adds signal to the rest of GRID//NODE.

Phase Engine

See educational protocol cycle visibility from your SHOTS history.

Phase Engine uses user-entered tracking data to estimate protocol progression, surface timing patterns, and support educational awareness without medical guidance.

RESULTS

Understand outcomes from your own recorded protocol history.

RESULTS turns logged SHOTS, weight, symptoms, and notes into outcome-tracking context over time.

LAB

Biotech utility tools for organizing protocol information.

LAB is a restrained utility layer for calculators, unit conversions, and educational tools—not medical direction.

NODE

Data-grounded pattern awareness across your personal biotech record.

NODE connects timing, tracking, outcomes, symptoms, and notes without becoming coaching or treatment guidance.

VAULT

Your secure personal data core.

VAULT keeps identity, protocol context, settings, and data ownership language tied to: Your body. Your data. Your grid.

FAQ

GRID//NODE begins with GLP-1 tracking and organizes user-entered records locally by default. Cloud recovery is optional.

BOUNDARY

GRID//NODE is a tracking and educational intelligence system.

GRID//NODE is not a doctor, diagnosis system, treatment system, prescribing tool, or dosing authority.

PRIVACY + DATA OWNERSHIP

GRID//NODE stores your record on this device by default. If you connect a cloud account, the workspace is associated with that account so recovery and synchronization can occur.

You can export your records and choose whether to use cloud recovery. GRID//NODE is not a diagnostic or clinical records service.

TERMS OF USE

GRID//NODE provides tracking and educational intelligence for user-entered records. You remain responsible for the accuracy of entries and for medical decisions.

Do not use GRID//NODE as a substitute for a licensed clinician, diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, or dosing guidance.
